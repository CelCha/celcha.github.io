Spartans Speak is a North Surrey Secondary's school magazine club. My role at the club was Head Designer of the magazine that we produced bi-monthly. I had full control over the design of the magazine. My duties included planning out the full layout of each issue, finding assets, taking photos, and deciding how articles are ordered. Management duties included making sure writers and editors submitted their articles on time. The magazines were designed with InDesign and Photoshop.



Duration: 3 years

Roles: Visual Design, Asset Creation, Graphic Designer, Photography

Tools: InDesign, Photoshop, Camera
