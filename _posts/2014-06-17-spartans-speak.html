Spartans Speak is a North Surrey Secondary's school magazine club. My role at the club was Head Designer of the magazine that we produced bi-monthly. I had full control over the design of the magazine. My duties included planning out the full layout of each issue, finding assets, taking photos, and deciding how articles are ordered. The magazines were designed with InDesign and Photoshop. You can view the November 2013 edition here.
